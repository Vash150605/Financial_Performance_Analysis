# Financial Performance & ESG Risk Analysis

> A junior data analyst end-to-end project exploring industry profitability
> and the relationship between ESG performance and net profit margins.

---

## Project Overview

This project analyses financial and ESG data across 8,909 companies,
6 industries, 3 regions, and 10 years (2015–2024) to answer two questions:

**Q1 — Which industries and regions have the highest revenue and net profit margins?**

**Q2 — Do companies with higher ESG scores tend to have better net profit margins?**
